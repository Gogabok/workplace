How to play Dice?

Determine a BET. This is the amount of TRX you will make bets on.

  • 1

    You can regulate the slider or enter the PREDICTION number on which you want to roll.

  • 2

    When you move the slider, your payouts and chances of winning will be arranged automatically.

  • 3

    Press ROLL to place a bet. Smart contracts will randomly generate LUCKY numbers from 0 to 99.

  • 4

    If the lucky number is higher your prediction, you WIN!

You can set automatic bets using the Auto button.

x50

Each round, player can place up to 5 bets on a single color or bet. Each color represents a multiplier and has its maximum bet restriction

If ring stops at the color player bet on, player will get reward at the corresponding multiplier. If the player loses, the bet amount will be deducted.

Space

Each time you play in Space, you must enter an auto multiplier value (Min 1.01, Max = 100,000/bet amount < 9999x). The Space multiplier value is independent and random between 1.00x and 9999x. The amount and/or # of players has no effect on the outcome.

Users also have the option to fix profit by clicking STOP. If the user does not manually escape, the auto stop function will kick in.

If you played successfully, payout will be according to the Space multiplier value. Loses will be deducted accordingly.

How to play Slots?

To start the game, we make a bet (bet amount) and choose the number of winning lines -the “lines”. The game uses game lines from 1 to 20, which increase the chances of winning.

When you press the “Spin” button, the reel starts spinning and falls out in random order.

Your winnings on each line will be summed up and displayed on the playing field in the line “Total winnings”

Provably Fair Betting

All bets on LEX games is placed through our smart contracts on the TRON.network blockchain. This ensures that LEX games is provably-fair. This is how it works:

You can enter your own SEED to the TRON.network. If you don't enter your own SEED, then a public random SEED will be used.

When the player places a bet and enters their own SEED, the smart contract will generate a random SHA-256 HASH, the timestamp of the current bet, and the previous blockhash from coinbase will be mixed to generate a random hash. The SEED generated from the smart contract and the SEED provided by the player will then be mixed to generate a random LUCKY number.
